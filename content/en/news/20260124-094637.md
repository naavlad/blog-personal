– The Clearing Company startup recently raised $15 million
– Coinbase Ventures was already one of the investors
– Coinbase previously launched predictions market direction
– The startup team will become part of Coinbase crypto exchange
– Predictions are available for sports, politics, and more
– The startup was founded by former Polymarket and Kalshi executive
– The deal will help Coinbase scale its new business
– The startup had about 10 employees in total
– Coinbase did not disclose the amount, calling it "immaterial"
– The acquisition became the tenth for the crypto exchange since early 2025
– Coinbase previously acquired Roam, Spindl, Iron Fish, Echo, and others
